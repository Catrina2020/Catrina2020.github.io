RCEP - 区域全面经济伙伴协定

After eight years of negotiations, 15 Asia-Pacific countries on Sunday signed the Regional Comprehensive Economic Partnership , the world’s biggest trade pact, a move considered a massive coup for regional economic integration, multilateralism and free trade.

Regional Comprehensive Economic Partnership，which is referred to as RCEP，covers a market of 2.2 billion people , or almost 30% of the world’s population,with a combined GDP of $26.2 trillion, or almost about 30% of global GDP. At the same time, it is considered to be the largest trade agreement in human history.

It is worth noting that this trade agreement was initiated by ASEAN, and China was only a later member country. However, to some extent, China, Japan and South Korea, through the RCEP platform, implicitly reached the trade cooperation previously blocked by the United States.

The agreement “is critical to the region’s response to the COVID-19 pandemic and will play an important role in building the region’s resilience through an inclusive and
sustainable post-pandemic economic recovery process,” leaders said in the statement.
